LEARN ABOUT TOTAL KNEE REPLACEMENT SURGERY (TKR)

Knee replacement surgery (arthroplasty) involves replacing damaged, worn, or diseased knee with an artifical joint/prosthesis.

The doctor may recommed total knee replacement surgery to manage the following health conditions.

  • Osteoarthritis
  • Rheumatoid arthritis
  • Haemophilia
  • Gout
  • Unusual bone growt
  • Knee injury
  • Knee deformity and loss of cartilage
  • Death of knee joint bone

Total knee replacement surgery has manifold benefits. Primarily, it offers complete relief from symptoms and promotes the health of your knee joint, in the long run. Some common advantages of total replacement include:

Pain relief
Improved mobility
Improved quality of life

KNOW MORE ABOUT KNEE REPLACEMENT SURGERY

  • . What is Total knee replacement surgery or TKR ?

Knee replacement surgery (arthroplasty) involves replacing damaged, worn, or diseased knee with an artifical joint/prosthesis.

It is recommended when a knee injury or arthritis (particularly osteoarthritis) damages or inflames the knee, thus limiting your ability to flex and extend the knee. As a result, you may face difficulty in performing basic daily activities like sitting and lying down.
